  • To create and manage your UnlimData account

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share data only with Paystack (payment processing) and network providers (phone number only, to deliver your data plan).

All data is stored securely. Passwords are never stored in plain text. Payment information is handled entirely by Paystack and never touches our servers.

We use minimal browser storage to keep you signed in and remember preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.
